About Me

I am a student at the University of Waterloo. I enjoy working with technology, attending hackathons and being a leader in my community. You can talk to me about meeting new people, dancing or books.

Here are the great places I've worked at:

As an Android developer for TD Bank Innovation lab, I worked with other students to build banking solutions. I worked on everything from brainstorming and ideation to prototyping an android app.

I had the opportunity to travel to beautiful Victoria, British Columbia to work for Sendwithus. Sendwithus is a tool to send email from your application. I contributed to the website development and video marketing.

At Agriculture Canada in Ottawa, Ontario I worked on website analytics for the IT Team. I dabbled with building a web search engine and doing testing for various systems.

My incredible volunteer experiences:

While working for Sendwithus in Victoria, I was introduced to Ladies Learning Code. I mentored women who were transitioning into tech careers to learn HTML & CSS. My favourite experience was teaching the Girls Learning Code workshop for 7-12 year olds. The honor of empowering girls to translate their dreams of one day owning a horse ranch or a bakery into a website was unforgettable.

The Waterloo Food Bank services local programs and families with non-perishable and sustainable foods. I help them as an ambassador during events and raise awareness for ending hunger.
